PRIVACY POLICY AND TERMS OF USE - MG: Mis gastos,Cuentas claras

Last updated: January 16, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION

Welcome to MG: Mis gastos,Cuentas claras. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application MG: Mis gastos,Cuentas claras (available on iOS, iPadOS, and watchOS).

By using the application, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1. Financial and Usage Information

The application collects and stores data that you voluntarily enter, such as:
- Budget sheet names.
- Expense and income records (amounts, categories, dates).
- Images or files attached to your transactions.

2.2. Synchronization Data (iCloud)

To provide a seamless experience across your devices, MG: Mis gastos,Cuentas claras uses Apple iCloud services. Your financial data is stored in your private iCloud container and synchronized across your devices (iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple Watch). We, as developers, do not have direct access to this data; it is encrypted and managed by Apple.

2.3. Biometric Data (Face ID / Touch ID)

If you enable the app lock feature, MG: Mis gastos,Cuentas claras uses Apple’s Local Authentication APIs (LocalAuthentication). The application only receives a “success” or “failure” confirmation from the operating system. We never collect, store, or have access to your biometric data (fingerprint or facial data).

2.4. Advertising Information (AdMob)

MG: Mis gastos,Cuentas claras uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may collect and use device identifiers (such as the IDFA) and approximate location data to provide personalized and relevant ads.
- For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

3. USE OF INFORMATION

We use the collected information to:
- Allow you to manage your expenses and budgets.
- Synchronize your information across your devices via iCloud.
- Protect access to the application through biometric authentication.
- Display advertising through Google AdMob.
- Improve the functionality of the application.

4. SHARING INFORMATION WITH THIRD PARTIES

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We share information only in the following circumstances:

- Service Providers: Such as Google AdMob (for advertising) and Apple iCloud (for storage and synchronization).
- Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights.

5. DATA SECURITY

We take reasonable measures to protect your information.
- Local and Cloud Storage: Your data resides on your device and in your personal iCloud account.
- App Lock: We offer an additional layer of security through passcode and biometric authentication to restrict access to the application on your device.

However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. YOUR RIGHTS

You have control over your data:
- Access and Editing: You can view and edit your financial data directly within the application.
- Deletion: You can delete sheets, expenses, or attachments at any time. If you delete the application and your iCloud data, the information will be permanently removed.
- Privacy Settings: You can manage advertising tracking permissions (ATT) in your iOS device settings.

MG: Mis gastos,Cuentas claras is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that we have collected information from a child without verified parental consent, we will delete that information.
